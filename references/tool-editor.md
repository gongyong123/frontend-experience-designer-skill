# Tool Editor

Goal: preserve flow for users creating, editing, arranging, or configuring.

## Visual Rules

- Keep toolbars stable.
- Make selection, drag, resize, snap, undo, and save states obvious.
- Prioritize canvas/work area over decoration.
- Use predictable panels and inspectors.

## Motion Rules

- Motion intensity should be low to medium.
- Use quick tactile feedback for direct manipulation.
- Animate drag previews, drop targets, snap guides, panel open/close, and selection outlines.
- Avoid page-level motion during active editing.

## Good Patterns

- Selection ring and resize handles.
- Drag ghost and drop target highlight.
- Inspector panel slide/fade.
- Undo/redo feedback.
- Save state transition: saving, saved, error.

## Avoid

- Moving toolbars.
- Hover effects that change control size.
- Long transitions between editing modes.
- Background animation that distracts from precision work.
